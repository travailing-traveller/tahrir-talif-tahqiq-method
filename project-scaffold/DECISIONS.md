# Editorial Decisions (Taḥqīq log)

Record a decision only when it is **hard to reverse**, **surprising without
context**, and **the result of a real trade-off**. One short entry each: what was
decided and why. Newest at top.

> Example shape:
> **[{CREATED_DATE}] Base text** — We follow [edition] for pagination and the
> [riwāya] reading, because [reason]. Variants handled per [policy].
