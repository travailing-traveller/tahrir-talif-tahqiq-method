# Sources

Every source the project rests on. The **handle** is the corpus filename stem
(`corpus/<handle>.txt`) that spans cite. **Trust** is your honest assessment of
the copy you hold — not the work's authority, but the *edition/file's* reliability.

| Handle | Work / edition | Location in corpus | Retrieved? | Trust | Notes |
|--------|----------------|--------------------|-----------|-------|-------|
| sample-source | (illustrative placeholder) | corpus/sample-source.txt | yes | n/a | demo only — delete |

Trust levels (suggested): `critical-edition` > `vetted-digital` >
`ocr-corrected` > `ocr-raw` > `unverified`. A faithful copy of a faulty edition
is still faithful to the wrong thing — record what you actually have.
