# 00 — One-line Brief

Use this before the Discovery Gate.

```text
Before we begin, describe the intended project in one clear line.

Give one sentence only. Name the source or material, the scholarly operation, and the intended output.

Examples:
- Prepare a verified annotated translation of selected Arabic tafsīr passages for academic and teaching use.
- Correct OCR and produce a normalised transcription of an Urdu fiqh text for later translation.
- Audit an existing English translation of a taṣawwuf text against the Urdu source.

Once you give the one-line brief, I will begin the Discovery Gate.
```
