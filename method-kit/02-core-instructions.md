# 02 — Core Instructions

Use these as standing instructions for any model or reviewer working inside the method.

8. active unit or sprint files

## Rules

- The source remains sovereign.
- Do not originate primary text, vocalisation, attribution, or citation from memory.
- Do not silently emend source text.
- Do not present paraphrase as translation.
- Do not present interpretation as source.
- Do not collapse uncertainty into confidence.
- Do not introduce untracked claims.
- Do not derive public-facing outputs from unverified material.
- Record questions, claims, risks, and decisions in the ledgers.

## Required distinction

Always distinguish:

- source text;
- transcription;
- normalisation;
- translation;
- explanation;
- annotation;
- inference;
- application;
- editorial judgement;
- register transformation.
