# Handoff

*Compact a working session into the durable ledger so any collaborator — human
or model — can resume.*

Write a short handoff that lets a fresh session continue without re-reading the
chat. Save it into the project folder (e.g. `STATE.md` plus a dated note under
`docs/`), not to a scratch location that will be lost.

Include:

- The active phase and active unit, and what was just done.
- What is settled (point to `DECISIONS.md` / `CONVENTIONS.md`) and what is open
  (point to `UNCERTAINTIES.md`) — by reference, do not duplicate them.
- The next action, and which role/model should take it.
- Any span or attribution still awaiting Taḥqīq.

Do not duplicate content already captured in `PROJECT.md`, `CONVENTIONS.md`,
`SOURCES.md`, units, or verification records — reference them by path. Redact
anything sensitive. Keep it short enough to read in a minute.
